About Our School

Welcome to UJJAVAL PUBLIC SCHOOL

Ujjaval Public School is dedicated to nurturing young minds by providing quality education in a safe, disciplined, and inspiring environment.

Our institution believes that every child possesses unique talents and abilities that should be encouraged through innovative teaching methods and holistic development.

Since its establishment, our school has focused on academic excellence, moral values, leadership skills, creativity and social responsibility.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become one of the most respected educational institutions by providing world-class education that develops intellectual curiosity, creativity, compassion and leadership among students.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide affordable quality education while maintaining high academic standards.

We encourage critical thinking, teamwork, discipline, respect and ethical values in every student.

Core Values

  • ✔ Discipline & Integrity
  • ✔ Respect for Everyone
  • ✔ Innovation & Creativity
  • ✔ Leadership Development
  • ✔ Environmental Responsibility
  • ✔ Community Service

Why Choose UJJAVAL PUBLIC SCHOOL?

We combine experienced teachers, modern infrastructure, technology-enabled classrooms, sports facilities, co-curricular activities and value-based education for complete development of every child.
